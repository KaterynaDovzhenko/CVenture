FAQ

  • How do I book a camping adventure?

    Booking your adventure is simple! Navigate to the "Prices" section on our website and follow the easy steps provided. If you encounter any issues, our friendly customer support team is ready to assist.

  • What should I bring for my camping trip?

    Pack the essentials for a successful camping experience: clothing (weather-appropriate layers, waterproof gear, boots), ID, backpack, and your smile. Everything else we have! Tailor items based on location and weather. Contact us for any specific queries.

  • Are pets allowed at your campsites?

    Yes, we welcome furry companions at our campsites! We understand that pets are a part of your family and can enhance the outdoor experience. However, we kindly ask you to adhere to the following guidelines: leash policy, clean-up responsibility, and respect for quiet hours.

  • How are safety and security ensured at your campsites?

    Your safety is our top priority, and we have implemented various measures to ensure a secure camping experience: site monitoring, well-defined campground boundaries, emergency protocols, well-maintained facilities, lighting, educational resources, collaboration with local authorities, visitor guidelines.
